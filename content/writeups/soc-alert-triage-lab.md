## Lab context

- **Platform**: CyberDefenders  
- **Scenario**: SOC analysts receive multiple suspicious login alerts, some from foreign IPs.  
- **Goal**: Practice triage, enrichment, and escalation decisions.

## Walkthrough (high level)

1. Review identity provider logs for failed and successful logins.
2. Enrich suspicious IPs with threat intelligence.
3. Correlate events with VPN and endpoint logs.
4. Decide which alerts are false positives and which become incidents.
