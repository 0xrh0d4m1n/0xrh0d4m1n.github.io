# **SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response)**

> Technology that enables organizations to collect security threat data from multiple sources and respond to low-level security events without human assistance.

## **Core Capabilities**

- **Orchestration** — Integrating and connecting security tools and workflows.
- **Automation** — Executing predefined playbooks automatically.
- **Response** — Containing, mitigating, and remediating incidents.
- **Case Management** — Tracking incidents through their lifecycle.

## **SOAR vs SIEM**

| | SIEM | SOAR |
|-|------|------|
| **Focus** | Detection and alerting | Response and automation |
| **Input** | Logs and events | Alerts from SIEM and other tools |
| **Output** | Alerts and dashboards | Automated actions and playbooks |
